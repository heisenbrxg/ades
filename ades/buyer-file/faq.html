FAQ

What sectors does ADES specialize in?

ADES provides specialized MEP and engineering consultancy across commercial offices, high-rise residential buildings, industrial facilities, and healthcare sectors.

What levels of BIM LOD can you provide?

We provide Building Information Modeling (BIM) from LOD 100 (Conceptual) through to LOD 500 (As-built and Facilities Management), ensuring complete lifecycle support for your project.

Do you offer Energy Modeling and CFD analysis?

Yes, we perform advanced shadow analysis, energy modeling, and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) to predict airflow and temperature distribution, ensuring optimized design performance.

How does ADES ensure data security and coordination?

Utilizing our Integrated MEP approach and cloud-based BIM platforms, we ensure seamless coordination between all engineering disciplines while maintaining project data integrity.
