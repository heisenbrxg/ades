TPI Composites

40.5% COST REDUCTION

Massive Energy Savings through Value Engineering

ADES delivered an industry-leading success story for TPI Composites by fundamentally rethinking the plant's cooling infrastructure. Through detailed 3D BIM coordination and thermal load calculations, we achieved unprecedented efficiencies.

The Optimization

A conventional design would have required 4,000 TR of cooling capacity. The ADES design optimized this to only **2,400 TR**, significantly reducing both initial capital expenditure and long-term energy costs.

Technical Highlight

Reducing Transformer infrastructure by 2,000 kVA through aggressive load balancing and efficiency measures.

Measurable Results

Our calculations resulted in a **40.5% reduction in total cost**, saving the client approximately **2,042 Lakhs INR**.
